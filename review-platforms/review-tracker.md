## Review generation (white-hat only)

- Ask happy patients in person to leave a Google review at the end of a visit.
- Provide a short link or QR code to the Google review form.
- Send a polite follow-up email or text after appointments (with consent).
- Make it easy: link directly to the review form.

## Responding to reviews

- Respond to every review, positive and negative, within a few days.
- Thank reviewers by name where appropriate.
- For negative reviews: stay calm and professional, do not share any patient health details, and move specifics offline (invite them to call the office).

## Compliance notes

- Do NOT buy, incentivize, or fake reviews - this violates platform policies and can get the profile penalized.
- Never disclose protected patient information in a public reply.
- Keep the # of reviews used in schema (schema-markup repo) in sync with the real Google count.
