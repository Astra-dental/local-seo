# Published Backups

This folder stores point-in-time snapshots of live local-SEO assets for Astra Dental Centre, so there is a record of what was published and a reference to restore from.

## What to back up here

- Final Google Business Profile description and services list (as published).
- Screenshots or exports of completed GBP, Apple Maps, and Bing Places listings.
- A snapshot of the citation tracker at major milestones.
- Monthly action plans once completed.

## Naming convention

Use the asset and date, for example:

- `gbp-description_2025-01-15.md`
- `citation-tracker_2025-Q1.md`

## Notes

- Capture a fresh backup after any major NAP, hours, or listing change.
- Do not store login credentials, verification codes, or account passwords here.
- This is a record-keeping folder, not a place for sensitive account data.
