# Bing Places Notes

Bing Places (bingplaces.com) is the equivalent of Google Business Profile for Microsoft Bing. It also feeds business data used by some other services, so a claimed, accurate listing is worth maintaining.

## Setup checklist

- [ ] Sign in / claim the listing (owner uses a Microsoft account).
- [ ] Check whether a listing can be imported directly from Google Business Profile (Bing offers this).
- [ ] Verify ownership (Bing’s verification steps).
- [ ] Business name: Astra Dental Centre.
- [ ] Address matches canonical NAP (Unit 120, 20061 Fraser Hwy, Langley, BC V3A 0R4).
- [ ] Map pin accurate.
- [ ] Phone: 604-533-8806.
- [ ] Website: https://astradentalcentre.com/.
- [ ] Category: Dentist.
- [ ] Hours: Mon & Fri 10:00-19:00; Tue-Thu & Sat 09:00-17:00; Sun closed.

## Enhancements

- [ ] Add logo and photos.
- [ ] Add the business description and services.
- [ ] Keep hours and holiday hours current.

## Notes

- Importing from Google Business Profile is the fastest way to keep the two consistent - do this if available.
- Account claiming/sign-in must be done by the business owner.
- Re-check after any NAP or hours change.
