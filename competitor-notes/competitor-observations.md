# Local Competitor Observations

Notes on other dental practices competing for local visibility in Langley. The goal is to learn what is working for top-ranking local competitors, not to copy or disparage them. Fill in specific competitors after a local search audit.

## How to identify competitors

- Search the priority terms (e.g. "dentist langley", "invisalign langley") from a Langley location.
- Note which practices appear in the Google map pack (top 3) and on organic page 1.
- These map-pack practices are the real local competition.

## Things to compare

- Number and recency of Google reviews (a common ranking gap).
- Completeness of their GBP (photos, posts, services, Q&A).
- Which services they highlight and the keywords in their site content.
- Whether they have dedicated service-area or service pages.
- Citation footprint (how many directories list them).

## Turning observations into actions

- If competitors have far more reviews, prioritize review generation (see review-platforms/).
- If they have richer GBP profiles, close those gaps (see google-business-profile/).
- If they rank for terms we do not, consider new service or location pages.
