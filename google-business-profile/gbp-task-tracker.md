# Google Business Profile (GBP) Task Tracker

Tasks for optimizing and maintaining the Astra Dental Centre Google Business Profile. A complete, active GBP is the single biggest driver of local map visibility.

## Profile setup checklist

- [ ] Business name exactly "Astra Dental Centre" (no keyword stuffing).
- [ ] Primary category: Dentist.
- [ ] Secondary categories as relevant (e.g. Cosmetic Dentist, Emergency Dental Service).
- [ ] Address matches canonical NAP (see nap-consistency/nap-reference.md).
- [ ] Service area / map pin verified as correct.
- [ ] Phone: 604-533-8806.
- [ ] Website: https://astradentalcentre.com/.
- [ ] Hours set: Mon & Fri 10:00-19:00; Tue-Thu & Sat 09:00-17:00; Sun closed.
- [ ] Special/holiday hours kept up to date.
- [ ] Business description written (services, experience, location).
- [ ] Opening date / years in business added.
- [ ] Services list populated (Invisalign, implants, whitening, CEREC, etc.).
- [ ] Attributes set (e.g. wheelchair accessible, accepts new patients).

## Photos & media

- [ ] Logo uploaded.
- [ ] Exterior photos (so patients can find the building/parkade).
- [ ] Interior photos.
- [ ] Team / dentist photos.
- [ ] Add new photos regularly.

## Ongoing tasks

- [ ] Post a Google Update at least 1-2x per month (offers, tips, news).
- [ ] Respond to ALL reviews (positive and negative) promptly and professionally.
- [ ] Monitor and answer Q&A on the profile.
- [ ] Keep the booking link current.
- [ ] Check Insights monthly (searches, calls, direction requests).

## Notes

- Only the verified business owner/manager should edit the profile - direct the client to make changes in their GBP dashboard.
- Do not buy or incentivize reviews; this violates Google policy.
- Flag any duplicate or unauthorized listings for removal.
